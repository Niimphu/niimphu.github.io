Projects

For the Yogscast Jingle Game Jam 2024, I designed gameplay and wrote code for "Avarice", a 2D shooting game where you fight demons and try to collect money. The game features a unique mechanic where money is needed for all player actions; reloading, dodging, and staying alive. We won the award for "Best Use of Theme", and managing a group of strangers from the internet from different time zones was a fun new challenge.
